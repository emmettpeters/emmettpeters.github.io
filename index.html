Business and education

It's dangerous business describing yourself for an employer or anyone else for that matter, but here goes. I'm an extremely goofy and self-confident country kid from Castroville, Texas. I spend my off-time working outside as much as possible on different projects I come up with. Ever since I first started school I have both enjoyed and been quite apt at math and sciences. After highschool I worked for a small construction/HVAC company and learned everything I needed to build any sized building from the ground up (foundation,framing,electrical,ac,plumbing...etc). As of 2014 I am debt free and have been investing most of my disposable income in whatever I feel will make me some money. Most recently it has been buying, fixing up, and reselling used cars and properties. My last two vehicles I've flipped have sold for over 2.5 times what they were bought for. Now that I have a bit more capital behind me, a friend and I are looking into trading both International and Cryptocurrencies. I have been interested in coding and programming ever since I did an internship at SouthWest Research and I was exposed to just how many different problems can be solved and things can be done with even a low-level of understanding. Now, going through CodeUp, I'm learning that my passion for coding is growing rapidly as I almost can't help but learn something new and fascinating every day. I cannot wait to see where this knowledge takes me and all the new people I'll meet.
